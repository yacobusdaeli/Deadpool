DEADPOOL I (2016)
Genre : Action, Comedy
Director : Tim Miller.
Writers : Rhett Reese, Paul Wernick
"Deadpool," the 2016 film, tells the origin story of Wade Wilson, a former Special Forces operative turned wisecracking mercenary. Wade's life takes a turn when he is diagnosed with terminal cancer. In a desperate attempt to cure himself and be with his girlfriend Vanessa Carlysle, he undergoes a rogue experimental treatment that triggers a mutation, giving him accelerated healing powers but also leaving him horribly disfigured.
